About Us

Rajdhani Events is not just a business for us — it’s a family tradition that started back in 1965 with our grandfather. At that time, event planning in Jaipur was new, and he was one of the first to bring it into the city. Today, our father carries forward that same vision, and we continue to make every event as special as if it were our own.

We plan all types of events — weddings, corporate meetings, birthdays, cultural programs — and we also specialize in government work and tenders. Whether big or small, every function is handled with care, honesty, and attention to detail.

You’ll always find us at our shop in the main market, not just online, so you know exactly where to reach us. Over the years, we’ve successfully organized 100+ events, and the trust of our customers has been our biggest reward.

Why people choose us:

  • In business since 1965 — trust built over generations
  • Quality work at affordable prices
  • We treat your function like our own family’s function
  • We believe an event is not just about decoration and arrangements — it’s about creating memories you’ll be proud of for years to come.
  • 50+ Years of Trust & Excellence
  • Serving Celebrations Since 1965
