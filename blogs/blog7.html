Luxury Destination Weddings in Jaipur: Palaces & Heritage Hotels

By Rajdhani Events • Updated 2025

Jaipur’s heritage charm, royal architecture, and warm hospitality make it India’s most loved destination for regal weddings. Here’s how to plan yours seamlessly.

Top Venues

Rambagh Palace: iconic lawns, Mughal gardens, and grand courtyards—perfect for pheras at sunset.
Samode Palace: frescoed halls and terraces for mehendi, sangeet, and intimate receptions.
Fairmont Jaipur: large capacity, Moroccan-Rajput blend, great for lavish baraats and sangeet nights.
City Palace / Jai Mahal: stunning backdrops for photos and royal processions.

Royal Baraat & Heritage Touches

  • Traditional entries: baggi (carriage), horse, shehnai & nagada, rose petal showers.
  • Décor palette: rani pink, marigold gold, emerald—paired with vintage lanterns and gota work.
  • Local experiences: folk dances (ghoomar, kathputli), live mehfil, and chaat counters.

Guest Logistics

  • Airport transfers, welcome desks, and hospitality teams at hotels.
  • Curated city tours (Amer Fort, Hawa Mahal, shopping at Johari/Tripolia bazaar).
  • Weather plan: tented backups for winter nights and monsoon showers.

Budgeting Smartly

  • Prioritize venue + catering + photography; reuse florals across functions.
  • Choose shoulder dates; weekday events are often more economical.
