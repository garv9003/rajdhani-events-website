Pre-Wedding Shoots: Top Locations in Rajasthan & Beyond

By Rajdhani Events • Updated 2025

From royal forts to serene lakes, Rajasthan is a dream for pre-wedding shoots. Here are our favorite spots—plus a few international gems for couples looking abroad.

Rajasthan Favorites

Amer Fort, Jaipur: grand arches, sandstone textures, sunrise light at the fort ramparts. Permits needed.
Pushkar Ghats: soft pastels, lake reflections, rustic alleys. Best at dawn.
Jodhpur Blue City: blue lanes and Mehrangarh views—perfect for contrast outfits.
Sam Sand Dunes, Jaisalmer: golden hour magic, silhouettes, and night sky shots.

Beyond Rajasthan

Bali: waterfalls, rice terraces, and cliffside sunsets.
Greece (Santorini): white-and-blue domes with Aegean backdrops.

What to Wear

  • Flowy outfits for movement; avoid heavy bling for long walks.
  • Complimentary colors to the location (pastels for forts, brights for dunes/blue lanes).
